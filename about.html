Our Story and Vegetarian Philosophy

Bansi Vihar Restaurant is a family-owned destination where traditional flavors, genuine hospitality, and community values come together.

Rooted in Patna, Built for Families

Founded with the dream of offering pure vegetarian meals in a warm, welcoming environment, Bansi Vihar has become a trusted dining spot for Patna families, office teams, and travelers. We celebrate the culinary heritage of Bihar and North India while honoring the comfort of South Indian classics.

Every dish is prepared in a clean vegetarian kitchen using traditional recipes, authentic spices, and a dedication to quality that keeps our guests returning.
